Problem : For secondary school students to do an exercise via online. As we know, many students complain about the hardcopy exercises that are always missing and broken. So, HEBAK applications provide several lists of exercises for each subject in secondary school for students to enhance their lesson in school with up-to-date questions depending on the syllabus at school and with a more advanced features that will help them for further education like universities.

Our Project propose 4 features whcich is Login/Signup Page and Home Page, Resources Page, University Matching Page, and Exercises Page. All this features is proposes for making the user use our website easily

The Login/Signup Page is a critical feature of the website, providing a secure and personalized experience for high school students. By creating accounts, students can securely access the platform, ensuring their personal information and academic progress are protected. The login functionality allows returning users to seamlessly continue their educational journey, while the signup process introduces new users to the array of resources and tools available. This feature is fundamental in maintaining user-specific data, enabling personalized content delivery, and fostering a sense of ownership and continuity in the students' learning process.

The Home Page acts as the central hub of the website, welcoming students and providing easy navigation to all other sections. It is designed to be user-friendly and engaging, displaying important announcements, updates, and highlights of new or popular resources. The Home Page serves as the starting point for students to explore the platform, offering quick access to educational materials, exercise pages, and university matching tools. By presenting a clear overview and intuitive access points, the Home Page ensures students can efficiently navigate the site and make the most of the resources available to them.

The Resources Page is a comprehensive library of educational materials curated to support high school students in their studies. It includes textbooks, reference books, study guides, and other learning aids organized by subjects and topics. This page aims to be a one-stop resource center where students can find reliable and high-quality academic content to enhance their learning experience. By providing easy access to essential study materials, the Resources Page helps students deepen their understanding of various subjects, prepare for exams, and supplement their classroom learning with additional information and practice.

The University Matching Page is designed to help students set realistic academic goals and identify the best-fit universities based on their aspirations and academic performance. This feature offers personalized recommendations by analyzing students' strengths, interests, and current academic standing. It provides detailed guidance on the scores and skills needed to gain admission to specific universities, helping students focus their efforts on achieving their desired outcomes. By offering tailored advice and actionable insights, the University Matching Page empowers students to make informed decisions about their future education and career paths.

The Exercises Page offers a variety of practice exercises, including past year questions and problems from exercise books, to help students prepare effectively for exams. This feature allows students to test their knowledge, practice problem-solving skills, and track their progress over time. The exercises are designed to mimic real exam conditions, providing a realistic practice environment that can help reduce test anxiety and improve performance. By offering a wealth of practice opportunities, the Exercises Page supports students in reinforcing their learning, identifying areas for improvement, and achieving academic success.
